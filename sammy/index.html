Who We do

Provide simplified digital learning tools for rural women led MSME’s.
Provide financial counseling and access to financial services/loans to women led micro SME’s.
Provide specialized skill training and simplified business development tools to women led MSME’s in rural Nigeria.

Our Vision

To economically empower rural women, by bridging the gap in financial inclusion.

Our Mission

To strengthen the capacities of women led micro SME’s in rural Nigeria by bridging the gap in digital learning and financial inclusion.

Offer
Digital Learning

through our online learning platform and local outreach programs,we collaborate with community influencers to provide internet enabled devices to rural women, with which they can access our tailored E-learning modules in idea development and innovation,busioness startup,growth strategies and sustainability.

Financial Services

At OLEJU we provide financial counseling services to rural women, to help them determine viable investment opportunities. We also provide financial support to viable MSME’s to help them launch and grow their enterprises.

Skill Training

Women from rural communities can access onsite specialized skill training, in order to upgrade their knowledge on previously learned skill or pursue new interests in vocational skills.

Business Development Tool Kit

OLEJU through years of experience working with small and micro enterprises, have developed a business development tool kit, which serves as a guide for small businesses to keep records and track their financial growth and investments. Our tool kit is simplified and tailored to help MSME’s imbibe book keeping culture in order to give their businesses a real chance at survival.
